Pat's Salmon Cookies

This store sells an interesting batch of cookies for people who are up for an adventure. These are called Pat's Salmon Cookies. With an new taste and a surprising twist, these salmon shaped cookies will leave you wanting more. These cookies have been made favorite by young, old, fit amd all other types of people all over the area.

History of the Company

Pat started this company about five years ago working in a little shop that he owned downtown at the corner of 1st and Pike. After being a great success, Pat decided to branch out and make more bakeries for his interesting cookies. He has set up bakeries in the SeaTac Airport, Seattle Center, Capitol Hill and Alki. Unfortunately we don't have an online order system. you will have to come and visit us if you would like one of these deliciously interesting salmon cookies.
